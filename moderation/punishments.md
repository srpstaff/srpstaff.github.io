# In-Game Punishments Guidelines

Consistent punishment enforcement ensures fairness across the server. Use this scale as a standard reference for common rule violations.

### Punishment Escalation
1. **Minor Infractions (SPAM, Minor Caps, Minor Disrespect):**
   * 1st Offense: Verbal Warning / `:warn`
   * 2nd Offense: Temporary Kick / `:kick`
   * 3rd Offense: Temporary Ban (1–3 Hours)

2. **Moderate Infractions (VDM, Minor Troll, Chat Trolling):**
   * 1st Offense: `:kick` or 3-Hour Ban
   * 2nd Offense: 24-Hour Ban
   * 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

3. **Severe Infractions (Exploiting, Severe Toxicity, Racist Slurs, Mass RDM):**
   * 1st Offense: **Immediate Permanent Ban** (No warnings required)
