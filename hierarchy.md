# Staff Hierarchy & Structure

Our staff team is split into distinct tiers to ensure efficient game management and support.

* **Trial Moderator (T-Mod):** Entry-level staff under supervision. Responsible for learning commands, handling basic reports, and shadowing senior staff.
* **Moderator (Mod):** Core moderation unit. Independently handles game chats, standard rulebreakers, RDM/VDM reports, and player requests.
* **Senior Moderator (Sr. Mod):** Experienced staff trusted with complex sits, heavy traffic control, and assisting lower ranks.
* **Human Resources (HR / Admin Team):** Manages staff logs, tracks activity quotas, handles staff complaints, hosts training sessions, and oversees promotions.
* **Management / Executive Team:** High-command control over server configurations, global bans, alliances, and core development.
