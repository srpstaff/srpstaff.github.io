# Staff Code of Conduct

As a member of our moderation and HR team, you represent the foundation of our community. Your behavior sets the standard for all players.

### Core Rules
1. **Professionalism:** Maintain a calm, neutral, and mature attitude, especially during high-stress arguments or ticket scenarios. Do not argue back with players.
2. **Grammar & Communication:** Use clear, proper grammar and spelling while handling moderation reports or speaking in official server areas. 
3. **Zero Bias:** Treat all players equally. Never show favoritism to friends, server boosters, or fellow staff members.
4. **Off-Duty Conduct:** While you have more freedom off-duty, severe toxicity, racism, or breaking Roblox Terms of Service (TOS) will result in immediate termination from the staff team.
