# Strike & Appeal System

Accountability is key to a healthy staff team. If rules are broken, the HR team utilizes a formal strike framework.

### Strike Framework
* **Strike 1:** Written warning and formal notification in staff logs.
* **Strike 2:** Temporary demotion or suspension for 3 days.
* **Strike 3:** Immediate removal from the staff team and permanent blacklist from applying again for 30 days.

### How to Appeal a Strike
If you believe a strike was issued unfairly, you may open a confidential ticket with the Lead HR Manager. Provide screenshot or video evidence supporting your case within **48 hours** of receiving the strike.
