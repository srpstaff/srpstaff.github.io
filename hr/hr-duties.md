# Human Resources (HR) Internal Guidelines

As an HR team member, you hold a position of high trust. You are responsible for managing staff integrity, processing applications, and maintaining fairness.

### Core HR Responsibilities
1. **Confidentiality:** Never leak staff ticket discussions, internal HR debates, strike logs, or personal staff information to regular members or external players.
2. **Fairness & Impartiality:** When handling a staff report or complaint, investigate objectively using logs and evidence. Personal friendships must never influence your rulings.
3. **Activity Accountability:** HR members are expected to lead by example, maintaining high activity quotas and responding to staff tickets within **24 hours**.
4. **No Abuse of Power:** HR ranks must never use administrative or management permissions to bypass standard server rules or assist friends in-game unfairly.
