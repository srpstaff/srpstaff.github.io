# Resignation & Leave of Absence (LOA)

We understand that real-life commitments come first. If you need to step away or leave the team permanently, please follow these procedures.

### Taking a Leave of Absence (LOA)
* **Notice:** If you will be inactive for more than 7 days due to vacations, exams, or personal reasons, you must submit an LOA request in the staff communications channel at least 24 hours in advance.
* **Duration:** Standard LOAs are granted for a maximum of **14 days**. Extensions require approval from a Lead HR Manager.
* **Inactivity Without Notice:** Failing to log activity or notify HR for 7+ days without an approved LOA will result in an automatic removal for inactivity.

### Resigning from the Staff Team
* If you decide to resign, please open a ticket or notify HR privately. 
* Provide your exact departure date and a brief reason.
* Staff members who leave on **good terms** (with clean logs and proper notice) are eligible for rehire in the future. Leaving mid-shift out of anger or ghosting the team will result in a permanent staff blacklist.
